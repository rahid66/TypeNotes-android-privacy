# Privacy Policy for TypeNotes

Last updated: June 2026

## Overview

TypeNotes is a note-taking application that allows users to create, edit, organize, and store notes on their device.

## Information We Collect

TypeNotes does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information.

Notes created by users are stored locally on the device.

## Data Storage and Backup

Notes are stored on the user's device.

Android may back up app data through the user's Google backup settings. If backup is enabled by the user, notes may be restored when the application is reinstalled or when setting up a new device.

## Data Sharing

TypeNotes does not sell, rent, or share user data with advertisers or third parties.

## Advertising

TypeNotes does not display advertisements.

## Children's Privacy

TypeNotes is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.
